# Wellnza Nutrition — Brand Guide

---

## Brand Story

In Amravati, Maharashtra, two friends spent years training hard in the gym and even harder at their local supplement shops — haggling with smug retailers over imported tubs of Optimum Nutrition that cost more than a week's groceries. They'd flip the label, squint at the fine print, and still not know what half the ingredients actually were.

Meanwhile, the Indian brands were cheaper, sure. But flip *their* labels and you'd find maltodextrin masquerading as protein, creatine monohydrate cut with fillers, and marketing copy that read like it was written by someone who'd never actually stepped foot in a gym.

These two friends — let's call them the founders — were tired of it. Tired of paying import duties for products that were barely better than the local stuff. Tired of the lie that "premium" meant "foreign." Tired of Indian athletes getting second-rate options while paying first-rate prices.

So in 2024, they started Wellnza Nutrition.

The promise was simple and stupid-hard: make genuine, world-class sports nutrition right here in India. No imported markup. No hidden fillers. Clean labels you could actually read and trust. Price transparency — not the "manufacturer's suggested retail price" shell game that every other brand plays.

They found manufacturers who could meet international standards without the international logistics overhead. They personally tested every formulation with local athletes — not sponsored pros, not people on payroll, just regular gym-goers putting in real work. They baked the feedback in.

Today, Wellnza is the brand Indian athletes go to when they're done settling. Not because of marketing. Because when you read the label, what you see is what you get — and what you get is actually good.

This is nutrition built for champions. Not the champions who get free gear. The ones who show up every day and earn it.

## Brand Positioning

### WHO it's for
Serious Indian athletes — the 6am gym regulars, the powerlifters, the crossfit WOD warriors, the Muay Thai fighters, the college athletes balancing sport and studies. Not casual joggers or weekend walkers. The people who understand that what you put in your body matters, and who are fed up with brands that treat them like idiots.

### WHAT it delivers
Clean, transparent, genuinely premium sports nutrition — protein, creatine, pre-workouts, amino acids — made in India to standards that hold up against any imported competitor.

### HOW it's different
Every gram on the label is accounted for. No proprietary blends hiding the actual doses. No "matrix" formulas that bury the effective ingredient at the bottom of the ingredient list. No imported brand premium built on logistics and duties rather than actual quality. Indian manufacturing that punches above its weight — because it has to, because the founders and their friends actually use this stuff.

### WHY it matters
Indian athletes have been price-gouged and quality-pampered for too long. Either pay triple for imported products with maltodextrin as the third ingredient, or save money with local brands that are essentially fillers and flavoring. Wellnza is the third option: actually good, actually honest, actually made here.

## Tone of Voice

**1. Unapologetically Direct**
No corporate fluff. No "journey of holistic wellness." We say what we mean. If a product has 25g protein per serve, we say 25g — not "helps support protein intake." Athletes don't have time for euphemisms.

**2. Educating, Not Lecturing**
We explain the *why* — why creatine monohydrate is the most researched form, why you want high bioavailability in your amino acids, why "proprietary blends" are a scam. But we do it like a training partner who knows their stuff, not a textbook or a lecture. Respect the intelligence of the reader.

**3. Confident Without Being Arrogant**
We know what we make is good. We don't need to tear other brands down to prove it. No "other brands are garbage" energy — that's cheap. We let the label do the talking. But we also don't hedge. "Might help," "may support," "formulated to assist" — none of that wimpy language.

**4. Rooted in Reality**
We know what it's like to train in India — the power cuts, the shared gym floors, the monsoon season runs, the local food that doesn't match your macros. Our language reflects the actual Indian fitness experience, not some fantasy of a climate-controlled LA gym.

**5. Premium Without Being Pretentious**
"Premium" doesn't mean gold-plated packaging and celebrity endorsements. It means: the product works, the label is honest, the price is what it should be. We act like adults who understand value, not like a brand trying to impress anyone.

**6. Community-Owned**
This brand was built by gym-goers for gym-goers. Our audience is our peers, not consumers to be marketed at. Language is "we" and "us," not "you the customer." If something isn't right, we say so — about our own products too.

## Key Messages

### Primary Message
**Wellnza is the honest, premium option Indian athletes have been waiting for — built here, tested here, priced for here.**

### Supporting Messages

**On Quality:**
"Imported standards. Indian manufacturing. Zero compromise."
We meet global quality benchmarks — because our founders and their training partners actually use this stuff and need it to work.

**On Transparency:**
"What you read is what's in the tub."
No proprietary blends. Full dose disclosure. Every ingredient listed with its exact amount. If we say 5g creatine monohydrate, it's 5g of actual creatine monohydrate.

**On Price:**
"We cut the import duties, not the quality."
The reason imported brands cost more isn't quality — it's logistics, import duties, and brand margin. We make the same quality here without those costs.

**On Fit:**
"Built for the Indian athlete — in the gym, in the kitchen, in life."
Formulated for how Indians actually train, eat, and live. Not a copy of what works in America or Europe.

### Anticipated Objections & Responses

**"If it's Indian-made, how can it be premium?"**
India has world-class pharmaceutical and supplement manufacturing. The same facilities that produce for global brands produce for us — because quality manufacturing exists here. What India lacked was a brand with the integrity to use it without the imported label markup.

**"I've never heard of Wellnza. How do I trust you?"**
We're not a decade-old brand, true. But we've personally used every product we sell — our founders train with this stuff daily. And we'll tell you exactly what's in every tub, down to the gram. That honesty is worth more than a famous name.

**"MuscleBlaze/Optimum Nutrition has more options. Why should I switch?"**
More options isn't better — clearer options is. Look at their labels vs ours. See how much actual active ingredient is in their "proprietary blend." Compare the serving size to the tub size. Do the math. Then decide.

**"It's probably the same as other Indian brands, just marketed better."**
The label is the proof. We're not asking you to trust our marketing — we're asking you to read the facts. If another brand can show you a cleaner label at a better price, we'll tell you to buy it. We know we're not for everyone. But for athletes who want transparency and quality, we're exactly it.

## Brand Elements

### Color Palette

| Name | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| **Obsidian** | `#0B0F0C` | Primary background — creates dark luxury aesthetic, used on all major surfaces |
| **Charcoal** | `#141A16` | Secondary background, card surfaces, subtle elevation |
| **Forest Green** | `#14532D` | Dark green accent — used sparingly for depth and brand grounding |
| **Gold** | `#E8A020` | Primary accent — used for CTAs, highlights, key UI elements, gold diamond motif |
| **Cream** | `#F7F3EC` | Primary text — warm, readable against dark backgrounds |
| **Muted Gold** | `#B8872A` | Secondary gold for subtle use, borders, faint textures |
| **Smoke** | `#8A9A8E` | Secondary text, captions, metadata — muted against dark |

### Typography

**Headlines:** Rajdhani (Google Fonts)
Bold, geometric, slightly condensed — industrial feel with Indian design heritage. Used for H1-H3, hero text, section headers. Weight 600-700.
*Why Rajdhani: Its angular geometry echoes the precision of sports science; its Indian roots connect to the brand's origin.*

**Body:** Jakarta (Google Fonts)
Clean, modern, highly readable. Used for paragraph text, UI labels, descriptions. Weight 400-600.
*Why Jakarta: Neutral and contemporary without being generic — reads well at small sizes on dark backgrounds.*

**Accent/Numerics:** Rajdhani 700
For key stats, numbers, percentages — where impact matters. Large, bold Rajdhani commands attention.

### Visual Motifs

**Gold Diamond ◆**
The core brand symbol. Represents: quality (diamond standard), precision (geometric clarity), Indian heritage (faceted like traditional design). Used as:
- Section dividers
- List item markers
- Accent marks in headlines
- Brand mark when paired with wordmark

**Gradient Text**
Gold-to-muted-gold gradient on key headlines — communicates premium without being gaudy. Used sparingly: hero text, brand statement moments. Never on body copy.

**Dark Ground**
Dark backgrounds (#0B0F0C) are the canvas. Light text breathes against it. This isn't a "dark mode" aesthetic choice — it's intentional luxury signaling. The darkness makes the gold pop.

**Geometric Structure**
Clean grids, deliberate whitespace, structured layouts. Visual language of precision and science. No decorative excess — every element earns its place.

### Imagery Direction

**What We Use:**
- Close-up texture photography: protein powder granularity, supplement tubs in soft focus, gym equipment silhouette
- Warm, low-light gym environments: moody, serious training spaces, not clinical
- Real athletes in real training moments: Indian gym culture, not stock photo gym culture
- Handwritten or hand-drawn elements sparingly: to humanize and add authenticity
- Dark, desaturated tones with selective warm highlights (gold)

**What We Don't Use:**
- Stock photos of people smiling while holding supplement tubs (deeply unserious)
- Overly polished, airbrushed fitness model imagery
- Caucasian or non-Indian fitness influencer aesthetics
- Bright, neon, "energy drink" aesthetic
- Clip art, generic icons, or cartoon elements
- White/light backgrounds for lifestyle imagery (contrast with brand darkness)

## Competitor Positioning

### Competitive Landscape

| Brand | Position | Weakness |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **Optimum Nutrition (ON)** | Gold standard imported brand | Expensive due to import duties, uses maltodextrin as filler in some products, "premium" is built on brand, not formulation |
| **MuscleBlaze** | Mass Indian market brand | Cheap = cheap formulations, heavy reliance on flavor and packaging over substance, transparency improving but still trails |
| **BigMuscles Nutrition** | Aggressive marketing, mid-tier | Better than most Indian brands but inconsistent quality, proprietary blends in some products, hype over substance |
| **MyProtein** | Value imported + private label | Huge range but quality variance, labels hard to parse, not formulated for Indian context |
| **Healthfarm** | Premium Indian aspirational | Better packaging than formulation, pricing approaches imported without the quality justification |

### Wellnza's Distinct Claim

**"Honest formulation. Indian manufacturing. No imported tax."**

The competitors fall into two buckets: expensive imports with clean labels (ON, MyProtein) and cheaper Indian brands with questionable formulations (MuscleBlaze, BigMuscles). Wellnza occupies the third space — genuinely clean formulations at Indian prices. Not by cutting corners, but by cutting the import middleman.

### What Wellnza Has That Competitors Can't Claim

1. **Full dose transparency** — every ingredient at exact dose, no "proprietary blends"
2. **Indian athletes as formulators** — not imported formulas repackaged
3. **Actual value** — the cost savings of Indian manufacturing passed to the athlete, not pocketed as margin
4. **No international logistics premium** — no import duties or overseas shipping costs baked into the price
5. **Label honesty** — if it says 30g protein, the tub delivers 30g protein per serving, calculated correctly against actual scoop size

### Positioning Statement

Wellnza is for the Indian athlete who has done the math — who reads labels, compares serving sizes, and refuses to pay for marketing overhead disguised as quality. We are not the cheapest. We are not the most famous. We are the most honest.
