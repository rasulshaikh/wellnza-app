## Product
Wellnza Nutrition — premium Indian sports nutrition brand from Amravati, Maharashtra. Pre-workout, protein, mass gainer, omega-3 supplements. Clean formulas: no sugar tricks, no artificial colors, no proprietary blends. Honest fuel for Indian athletes.

## Register
brand

## Users
Indian fitness enthusiasts, bodybuilders, gym-goers aged 18–35. Aspirational but price-aware. Distrust imported brands for being overpriced, under-dosed, or loaded with fillers.

## Brand Voice
Bold. Direct. Maharashtra-rooted. Champions framing — not corporate wellness, not Western neon. A coach's voice: confident, terse, no fluff.

## Anti-References
Generic Western supplement neons (MuscleTech electric blue). Corporate beige wellness (Herbalife). Import-premium posturing (ON Gold Standard's clinical white). Loud Indian brands that scream with red and orange on black.

## Strategic Principles
- Made in India is identity, not limitation
- Ingredient transparency over marketing headlines
- "Built for Champions" — a mindset, not a sport
- Luxury feel, honest pricing — no compromise on either
